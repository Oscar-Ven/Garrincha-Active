# Garrincha Active — Product Overview

## Vision

Garrincha Active is a sports activity tracking, rewards, and community platform built to make physical activity more engaging, measurable, and socially connected. Named after the legendary Brazilian footballer, the platform celebrates athletic spirit at every level — from casual joggers to competitive football players. Our vision is to become the go-to platform for sports communities in the MENA region and beyond, turning everyday physical effort into recognized achievement.

---

## Target Audience

- **Amateur and semi-professional athletes** aged 16–45 who participate in football, running, cycling, fitness, and other sports activities.
- **Sports center operators and academies** seeking digital tools to manage attendance, run challenges, and reward their members.
- **Sponsors and brands** in the sports, nutrition, and lifestyle sectors looking for targeted engagement with an active demographic.
- **Community coaches and team leaders** who want structured challenge and tracking tools for their groups.

---

## Core Value Proposition

Garrincha Active bridges the gap between physical activity and digital reward. Players earn points for every session logged, challenge completed, or event attended. Those points unlock real-world rewards from sponsors — discounts, merchandise, free sessions, and more. The platform makes progress visible, competition friendly, and community central.

---

## Key Features Summary

**Activity Tracking**
Log runs, walks, cycling sessions, football training, matches, and custom activities. Each activity is reviewed and approved, maintaining data integrity and fairness across the leaderboard.

**Points and Levels**
Players accumulate points from activities, challenge completions, event attendance, referrals, and badge awards. Points drive level progression through Bronze, Silver, Gold, and Elite tiers, unlocking higher-value rewards and platform privileges.

**Challenges**
Structured challenges across multiple types — distance, active minutes, activity count, football training attendance, match count, and team or center-vs-center competitions. Challenges drive engagement, retention, and healthy competition between players, teams, and sports centers.

**Rewards and Redemption**
A sponsor-backed rewards marketplace offering discounts, merchandise, free training sessions, food and drink vouchers, tournament entries, sponsor vouchers, and VIP access. Players redeem rewards using earned points with full lifecycle tracking from redemption through to use or expiry.

**Social Feed and Teams**
A moderated activity feed where players share achievements, badge earns, challenge completions, and event registrations. Team functionality allows players to form groups, compete together, and build community around shared sporting goals.

**Events**
Sports centers publish and manage events — training sessions, tournaments, leagues, camps, and community events. Players register directly through the platform, earning attendance points and keeping their activity history unified.

**Badges**
Achievement badges reward milestones and special accomplishments, surfaced on player profiles and shared automatically to the social feed.

---

## Business Model Overview

**Sponsor Integration**
Brands pay to place rewards in the marketplace. Reward categories are mapped to sponsor inventory — a sports brand might offer merchandise vouchers, a nutrition company offers product discounts, a sports center offers free sessions. Sponsors gain direct, measurable exposure to an active and engaged user base with clear redemption analytics.

**Sports Center Partnerships**
Sports centers (academies, gyms, football clubs) operate as platform partners with their own admin role. They list events, run center-specific challenges, manage their member base, and optionally offer rewards. Centers benefit from digital tools that increase member retention and activity participation.

**Platform Growth**
Referral bonuses incentivize organic user growth. Leaderboards and challenge mechanics drive daily active use. The combination of progress tracking, social accountability, and tangible rewards creates a retention loop that sustains long-term engagement.

## Platform Roles

| Role | Responsibilities |
|---|---|
| **Player** | Log activities, join challenges, earn points, redeem rewards, follow other players, join teams, register for events. |
| **Center Admin** | Manage their sports center profile, publish events, create center-level challenges, review and approve member activities, view center analytics. |
| **Platform Admin** | Oversee the entire platform — manage users, moderate content, approve activities globally, configure rewards, manage sponsors, and maintain platform health. |
| **Sponsor Admin** | Manage sponsor profile and reward catalog, view redemption analytics, and configure voucher availability and limits. |

## Success Metrics

- **Monthly Active Players (MAP):** Total unique players logging at least one activity per month.
- **Activity Approval Rate:** Percentage of submitted activities that pass review — indicates data quality and community trust.
- **Points Redemption Rate:** Ratio of earned points redeemed against rewards — indicates reward catalog relevance.
- **Challenge Participation Rate:** Percentage of active players enrolled in at least one active challenge per month.
- **Sponsor Reward Conversion:** Number of reward redemptions per sponsor per month — the primary commercial metric.
- **Center Retention:** Month-over-month retention of sports center partners and their registered member bases.
- **Net Promoter Score (NPS):** Measured quarterly across player and center admin cohorts to track platform satisfaction.
